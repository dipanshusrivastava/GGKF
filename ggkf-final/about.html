About Gurukul Global Knowledge Foundation (GGKF)


With the motto that "The World is one Family," GGKF is dedicated to nurturing peace, harmony, and happiness by empowering people with knowledge, skills, and human values that align with the needs of the new knowledge society of the age of innovation.

Aims and Objectives

GGKF is committed to advancing the general public good, focusing on education, research, and community welfare. Our objectives include:

  • Conducting skill development programs in areas of high employability.
  • Undertaking research projects aligned with our mission.
  • Organizing and attending national and international conferences.
  • Awarding fellowships and scholarships to deserving individuals.
  • Collaborating with like-minded organizations on a global scale.

Proposed Journal

We plan to publish peer-reviewed journals covering various research fields. Some of our proposed journals include:

  • Blue Sky: Focused on air quality, atmospheric studies, and meteorology.
  • Scientific Horizons: A platform for multi-disciplinary research.
  • Vivechana: Dedicated to Vedic and Indic studies, and Indian culture.
