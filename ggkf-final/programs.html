Programs


The GGKF organizes workshops and training programs for various stakeholders and government officials. Some of them are mentioned below-

  • Training Program on Remote Sensing and GIS Techniques for Earth Sciences: This program focuses on the practical application of remote sensing and GIS technologies in Earth Sciences, enhancing participants' ability to analyze and interpret geospatial data.

  • GIS Techniques for Planners: Designed for urban and regional planners, this training provides in-depth knowledge of GIS tools and techniques to improve spatial planning and decision-making processes.

  • Big Data Analysis: This course equips participants with the skills to manage and analyze large datasets, utilizing advanced tools and methodologies to derive meaningful insights from complex data.

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning: Participants will learn about the fundamental concepts and applications of AI and ML, including hands-on practice with popular algorithms and frameworks.

  • IoT for Automations: Focusing on the Internet of Things (IoT), this training explores the integration and application of IoT technologies in various automation processes, enhancing efficiency and innovation.

  • Cyber Security Technologies: This program provides comprehensive training on the latest cybersecurity technologies and practices, preparing participants to protect digital infrastructures from emerging threats.
