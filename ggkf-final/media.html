Media and Reporting

The "New Horizons" media portal will serve as the outreach platform for GGKF, featuring various types of content:

Magazine

A monthly web-based magazine focused on Science, Technology, Environment, Education, and Innovation, with plans for future print editions. It will include articles and editorials from both internal and external contributors.

Daily News

Short news articles published on the portal daily.

Podcasts

Podcasts and series featuring stories and interviews, focusing on single topics or series.

Documentaries

Documentary videos, articles, and podcasts on specific topics.

Newsletter

A quarterly newsletter detailing the work plans and projects of GGKF.
